Famous Court Jesters

Throughout history, court jesters have entertained royalty with their wit and wisdom. Here are some notable ones:

  • Triboulet - Court jester to King Francis I of France
  • Will Sommers - King Henry VIII's beloved fool
  • Archibald Armstrong - Court jester to James I and Charles I of England
